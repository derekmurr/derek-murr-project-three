Type Scale Generator

Are you a developer, but you ain’t no designer? Do you need to style your text and just need it to look good but don’t want to think too hard about it? When designers start talking about “proportion” and “vertical rhythm” do you break out in hives? Type Scale Generator has got your back, friend. Just choose a scale and bam: you’ve got sizes for body copy and every level of heading that are gonna look great together. Then just copy those sizes into your project’s CSS, and get on with your life. Easy peasy.

Just choose a scale and a font style, and we’ll do the rest. Pro tip: the further down the list of scales you go, the more dramatic the difference between your body copy and headings. So choices further down are better if your headlines are short and punchy, while wordier sites should stay close to the top of the list!
