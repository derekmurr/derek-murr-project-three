Type ScaleGenerator!

Are you a developer, but you ain’t no designer? Do just need style your text and have it look good, but don’t want to think too hard about it? When designers start talking about “proportion” and “vertical rhythm” do you break out in hives? Type Scale Generator has got your back, friend.

A type scale, like in music, helps make sure things are harmonious. There are several different scales that over the years we've found work really well, and now they're all right here for you to use! Just choose a scale and a font, and bam: you’ve got sizes for body copy, captions, and every level of heading that are gonna look great together. Then just copy those sizes into your project’s CSS, and get on with your life. Easy peasy.

Just choose a scale and a font style, and we’ll do the rest. These scales are all based on the most commonly used ones in print design – most of them are based on music. Basically, just play around and see which option feels right for your project!

Pro tip: the further down the list of scales you go, the more dramatic the difference between your body copy and headings. So choices further down the list are better if your headlines are short and punchy, while wordier sites should stay close to the top of the list.

It’s as easy as 1-2-3!
